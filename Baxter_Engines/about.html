About Us

Baxter Engines is a small local engine and machine shop owned by Gary Baxter since 1980. There's no end to the variety of work we do in our shop. From small Kohler Engines to gigantic tractor engines, go-kart to stock car engines, we have rebuilt it all. The thing that separates us from other engine shops is the expert machine work we do in-between the dismantle and re-assembly of each engine. It might seem like there's a million different things that could go wrong on the inside, but we've seen it all. Check out the Services page to see the most common machine work we do, but don't hesitate to give us a call or stop in and see what we can do for you.
